Join us in making a difference. Volunteer with Queer Kenya and support the LGBTQ+ community.

Why Volunteer with Us?

Volunteering with Queer Kenya offers you an opportunity to make a positive impact in the LGBTQ+ community. You will gain valuable experience, meet like-minded individuals, and help us drive meaningful change.

Whether you are interested in advocacy, event planning, outreach, or providing support services, we have a place for you. Your skills and passion can help us achieve our mission to celebrate diversity and promote inclusivity.

Volunteer Opportunities

Event Planning

Help us organize and execute events that celebrate and support the LGBTQ+ community.

Community Outreach

Engage with the community to spread awareness and provide essential resources and support.

Advocacy

Advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and work towards creating an inclusive society.
